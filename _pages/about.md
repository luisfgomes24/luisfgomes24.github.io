I’m a Software Engineering Ph.D. student at Carnegie Mellon University and the University of Porto, focusing on **Artificial Intelligence for Software Engineering** (AI4SE) and developers' **productivity**. My research explores how we can make programming more intuitive, building better Coding Assistants and tools to reduce friction in the development process. 

I'm also a Visiting Assistant Professor at the University of Porto and I have experience working on diverse projects, from enhancing ML workflows in startups to developing systems for smarter road maintenance.

When I’m not working, I enjoy surfing, skateboarding, and spending time outdoors in Porto. It’s my way of finding balance and inspiration for the next big idea.

Feel free to connect or learn more about my work here!
